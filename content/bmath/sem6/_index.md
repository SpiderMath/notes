## Thoughts

A highly intriguing semester. Certainly a hectic end to the chaotic degree! Filled to the brim with many entrances, interwoven with a couple of absolute curveballs of assignments, examinations and (a couple of) interviews. The subjects were very intriguing, and the learning felt rather fun, also, I was able to finish this semester on a high after the steep fall off a cliff that was my second year. So, I am rather satisfied with my performance. Although, I did end up missing out on the distinction by a fraction, which is a bummer, I still am proud of myself for still improving to an extent.
